Frequently asked questions

When is my order recorded?

Your order is saved only after Paystack confirms payment and the server verifies the webhook.

Which payment methods are supported?

Card payments, bank transfer, USSD, and Apple Pay on supported Apple devices using Safari.

Can I check out without an account?

Yes. Guest checkout is enabled. Returning customers can create an account to view future order history.

How do I track an order?

Use the order tracking page with your order ID or tracking number plus the email used at checkout.
